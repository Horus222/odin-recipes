Cake Mix Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 package white cake mix
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Stir cake mix, oil, and egss together in a large bowl until comes together as a dough.
  3. Form dough into 1-inch balls and place 2 inches apart on ungreased baking sheets.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven, about 6 to 10 minutes, depending on how soft you like your cookies. Cool cookies on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely
